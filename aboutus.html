Algorithm Health

Algorithm Health was founded with the mission of enhancing diagnostic accuracy and treatment precision in healthcare through innovative technology solutions.

Leveraging a sophisticated combination of artificial intelligence and advanced rule engines, Algorithm Health has developed a unique model of "guided AI" designed to significantly improve medical diagnostic accuracy while minimizing clinical errors. Our comprehensive system goes beyond analysis of traditional laboratory testing by incorporating patient symptoms, medical history, and clinical context to enable more accurate and holistic diagnostic interpretations.

Our Story

Our Story

The company was founded by Dr. Sumanth C Raman, who brings over 25 years of specialized experience in healthcare IT and holds an M.D. in Internal Medicine. Our co-founders are accomplished data scientists with extensive expertise in machine learning, application development, and database management.

Algorithm Health is proudly incubated by the Healthcare Technology Innovation Centre at IIT Madras Research Park and is supported by the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MEITY) , Government of India, positioning us at the forefront of healthcare technology innovation.
