Care AI EMR

Care AI represents the next generation of Electronic Medical Records, seamlessly integrating cutting-edge artificial intelligence to transform how healthcare providers deliver patient care. This revolutionary platform combines ambient voice transcription that captures clinical encounters naturally, intelligent decision support that enhances diagnostic accuracy, and automated workflows that eliminate administrative burden—all while maintaining the highest standards of security and compliance.

With Care AI, healthcare professionals can focus entirely on what matters most: providing exceptional patient care while the system intelligently handles documentation, insurance processing, and clinical insights in the background.

Care AI EMR

The Next Generation of Intelligent Healthcare Solutions

Transform your healthcare delivery with Algorithm Health's comprehensive Native AI Electronic Medical Record
system - designed from the ground up with artificial intelligence at its core.

Redefining Patient Care with Intelligent Technology

Care AI EMR doesn't just store medical records—it actively enhances clinical decision-making, automates routine tasks, and creates personalized care pathways that align with best practice protocols. Our system represents the future of healthcare information management.

Seamless Ambient Listening & Real-Time Transcription

Our advanced Ambient Listening and Transcription feature seamlessly captures the natural conversation between doctors and patients — no interruptions, no extra steps. As the consultation unfolds, the system intelligently listens in the background, identifies the medical components of the dialogue, and instantly transcribes them into the Electronic Medical Record (EMR) in real time.

Multilingual Support

Built with robust multilingual support, the system works effortlessly across most major languages — and can even handle multi-language conversations where patients and doctors switch between languages. This powerful tool reduces documentation burden, saves time, and ensures clinical accuracy, allowing healthcare providers to focus more on patient care and less on typing.
